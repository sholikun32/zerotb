Preskripsi

Cluster Clustering Label Intervensi
Cluster1 High-risk patients with poor health and living conditions, including patients with poor hygiene, poor lighting, and poor ventilation. These patients may have completed TB treatment, but they require additional care to manage any ongoing healthcare issues. ✓ Intervesi Lingkungan, Pendampingan Kepada Masyarakat
Cluster2 Patients with moderate risk health and living conditions, including patients with poor hygiene or ventilation but with no significant overall health risks. These patients may be from low-income households but do not have any chronic health conditions that require ongoing medical attention. ✓ Intervensi Lingkungan, Pemberdayaan Peningkatan Penghasilan
Cluster3 Patients from low-income households with TB and poor living conditions, including patients with poor ventilation, low-quality housing, and no access to healthcare. These patients require significant support to manage TB, and their lack of resources is a major barrier to their care. ✓ Intervensi Lingkungan, Pendampingan Kepada Masyarakat, Pemberdayaan Peningkatan Pengahasilan, Diperlukan pemeriksaan dan Promosi kesehatan
Cluster4 Patients with poor health and living conditions, likely due to economic circumstances. These patients may have chronic health conditions, such as diabetes, that require ongoing medical attention. Their economic difficulties also lead to poor living conditions, such as low-quality housing or lack of access to healthcare. ✓ Intervensi Lingkungan, Pendampingan Kepada Masyarakat, Pemberdayaan Peningkatan Pengahasilan, Diperlukan pemeriksaan dan Promosi kesehatan
Cluster5 Patients with poor health and living conditions, likely due to poor ventilation. These patients may have completed TB treatment but require ongoing care to manage other respiratory health issues. Their living conditions, such as poor ventilation or exposure to air pollutants, pose significant risks to their health. ✓ Intervensi Lingkungan, Pendampingan Kepada Masyarakat, Pemberdayaan Peningkatan Pengahasilan, Diperlukan pemeriksaan dan Promosi kesehatan
Cluster6 Patients with poor health and living conditions, likely due to poor hygiene. These patients may have completed TB treatment, but their living conditions, such as poor water or sanitation infrastructure, pose ongoing risks to their health. Additional support is needed to address these living conditions and prevent future health risks. ✓ Intervensi Lingkungan, Pendampingan Kepada Masyarakat, Pemberdayaan Peningkatan Pengahasilan, Diperlukan pemeriksaan dan Promosi kesehatan, Perbaikan Ventilasi, Air bersih
